How to Recycle

Recycling is an essential practice to reduce waste and help the environment. It starts with sorting your trash into different categories such as paper, plastic, glass, and organic waste. Most cities provide designated bins for each type of waste, making it easy to dispose of your trash responsibly. Ensuring that each material is placed in the correct bin helps with the efficient processing of recyclables.

Be mindful of special disposal requirements for hazardous materials like batteries or electronics, as these items should not be placed in regular recycling bins. Instead, look for local drop-off points or special collection services. By recycling properly, you can contribute to reducing the overall waste in landfills and support sustainable waste management practices.
